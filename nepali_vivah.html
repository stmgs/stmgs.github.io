Nepali Vivah

Nepali Vivah is a wedding planner service application available in Android platform. NepaliVivah is the top Nepali social matrimony platform for people of Nepali origin to find, meet and marry Nepali women and men. You can find Nepali single men and women mostly from Nepal and Northern India (NepIndia).
