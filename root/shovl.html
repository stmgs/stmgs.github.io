shovl

clear your way...

Shovl is a mobile application that provide snow clearing service. This app only acts as a mediator between actual shoveler and the customer that needs their snow removed from their property.

Roles and responsibilitites

  • Participate in the development of the app along with other team members.

  • Design the UX for the app

  • Braistorm the app concept and collaborate with team members about the features that can be included in the app

  • Participate in the sprint meeting with team members and discuss about the roadblocks, updates since last meeting and feature to be added for next sprint.

  • Present the demo of the app in each 2 week sprint demo to the client(professor)
